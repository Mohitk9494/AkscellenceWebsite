Sitecore services

Sitecore is a customer experience management product with everything needed to create most powerful, relevant, and individualized customer experiences. It connects brands with their customers for lifelong relationships. Whether it is web content management, complete digital experience management or an integrated commerce platform Identify which products will best benefit your business. Sitecore can be deployed in a variety of environments, depending on your business and infrastructure needs.

Key features of Sitecore:

  • Context driven content for better customer engagement to enhance customer experience.
  • Context driven commerce to increase successful transactions and enable up-selling and cross-selling.
  • Strong features to address challenging multilingual and multichannel requirements.
  • Integrated commerce (Online + Offline store + Multi-country business + Multiple websites) using unified.
  • Contextual intelligence.
  • Machine learning engine designed to deliver real-time analytics across both native and 3rd party customer data.
